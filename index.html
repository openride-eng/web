Open Ride is a community driven ride-sharing platform for Singaporeans.

Our mission is to move Singaporeans around for a fraction of the cost of current ride hailing apps. Open Ride works like a co-op, both drivers and riders benefit from this because we are zero-commission. Drivers earn more, riders pay less. It's pretty great.

You can schedule rides or customize your preferences for female drivers. Plus, everyone can be a driver so you can get back your petrol costs travelling to CBD. We think the future of transportation in Singapore is carpooling. In fact, we're betting on it.
